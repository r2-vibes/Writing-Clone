# Voice Profile (Template)

> This file captures your unique writing voice, built from analyzing your published work.

## Status: Not Yet Trained

Share your writing samples to get started. The agent needs:
- **3-5 published articles, essays, or blog posts** (your best work)
- **Newsletter editions** (if you write newsletters)
- **Podcast transcripts** (if available — shows your spoken voice)
- **Book excerpts** (if applicable)

The more material you provide, the more accurate the voice profile becomes.

## What Gets Generated

Once trained, this file will contain:
- Your core identity as a writer (who you are on the page)
- Your tone signature (confident? measured? playful? urgent?)
- Your emotional register (how you convey emotion — through story? data? irony?)
- Your structural patterns (how you build arguments, open pieces, close them)
- Your vocabulary preferences (words you love, words you avoid)
- Specific examples from your work that illustrate each pattern

## How It Improves

Every time you accept or reject a suggestion, the profile gets more accurate. Rejections are especially valuable — they show where the agent's model of your voice is wrong.
